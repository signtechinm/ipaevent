5. Form Behavior

  • An email address and all organization-detail fields are required.
  • The source uses a multi-step flow with Next and Clear form actions.
  • The premium sponsorship section instructs the respondent to select one category.
  • The standalone sponsorship section allows one or more items to be selected.
  • The souvenir advertisement section allows exactly one option.
  • Payment is supported through account transfer or UPI/QR.
  • Final submission requires transaction details, payment proof, and declaration acceptance.
  • Required fields must be validated before continuing.
  • Entered values should be preserved between steps.
  • The official event logo should appear near the top.
  • The form must not request passwords or sensitive account credentials.

7. Recommended Portal Flow

Step 1: Organization Details

Collect email, organization name, correspondence address, authorized person's name and designation, and mobile number.

Step 2: Sponsorship Selection

Collect one premium package or allow multiple standalone items. For sessions and cultural events, collect quantity and the specific allocation. Assign all prices and totals automatically. Additional recommended fields are branding requirements, website, and organization profile.

Step 3: Souvenir Advertisement

Allow exactly one advertisement placement and assign its price automatically. Reserve limited cover positions only after availability and payment conditions are satisfied.

Step 4: Branding Assets

Collect the organization logo, profile or brochure, and advertisement artwork. Display file formats, size limits, and artwork dimensions.

Step 5: Billing and Payment

Collect billing details, payment method, transaction reference, payment proof, amount paid, and payment date. Display the managed PHARMA FIRST account details and UPI QR code. Set submitted manual payments to verification required until an administrator confirms them.

Step 6: Declaration and Review

Show a complete summary including payment details and receipt status. Require an accuracy and authorization declaration, capture communication consent, and provide sponsorship terms.

Step 7: Confirmation

Generate an application number, show confirmation, email the submission, notify administrators, and display payment status and support contacts.

9. Admin Requirements

  • Search, filter, view, and export applications.
  • Assign applications to sponsorship coordinators and record follow-ups.
  • Approve, reject, hold, or request changes.
  • Verify payments and issue invoices or receipts.
  • Review logos, profiles, and advertisement artwork.
  • Track branding commitments and delivery.
  • Flag duplicate organization applications.
  • Maintain an audit trail of status and payment changes.

10. Recommended Status Values

Application: draft, submitted, under_review, changes_requested, approved, on_hold, rejected, completed, cancelled.

Payment: not_required, pending, partially_paid, paid, verification_required, failed, refunded.

11. Content and UX Recommendations

  • Use the consistent event name throughout the form.
  • Present the theme once without duplicated quotation marks or labels.
  • Use a multiline address field and country-aware telephone input.
  • Separate the authorized person's name and designation in storage.
  • Replace the five required Yes/No checkbox groups with one required package selector.
  • Format currency consistently without spaces inside the number.
  • Show premium and standalone sponsorship as clear routes.
  • Collect quantity for per-session and per-event items and calculate totals automatically.
  • Present Already a Premium Sponsor separately from priced standalone items.
  • Use a single-choice control for souvenir advertisements and show placement availability.
  • Do not expose the bank customer ID; show only payer-required details.
  • Provide copy controls for the account number and IFSC.
  • Separate transaction reference and date, correct receipt wording, and validate amount as numeric.
  • Use one required declaration checkbox instead of Yes/No choices.
  • Clearly mark required and optional fields.
  • Make the form mobile-friendly, keyboard accessible, and draft-safe.
  • Include a privacy notice, sponsorship terms, and support contact.

12. Open Items to Confirm

  • Final brochure content, package benefits, taxes, and GST treatment.
  • Availability and allocation rules for exhibition space, meals, bags, sessions, workshops, and cultural events.
  • Whether premium sponsors may also purchase standalone items.
  • Whether standalone items are exclusive or shared.
  • Quantity details and Other-option approval rules.
  • Advertisement artwork specifications, deadline, and cover-position reservation rules.
  • Final UPI ID, approved QR asset, partial-payment policy, and verification turnaround time.
  • Final declaration wording, legal/privacy review, and confirmation message.
  • Payment method, gateway, and required billing documents.
  • Upload formats, dimensions, and file-size limits.
  • Approval workflow, cancellation policy, refund rules, and responsible committee members.
  • Final support contacts and notification recipients.
